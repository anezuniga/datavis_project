Zoning reform in Metro Boston is vital for tackling systemic inequities and advancing environmental justice. By reevaluating policies like single-family zoning, which historically segregate and limit housing access for marginalized communities, we can break down barriers disproportionately affecting these populations. Our project uses data-driven visuals to underscore the urgency of zoning reform in promoting environmental justice. As discussions on the H.3138 Affordable Homes Act unfold, our visualization aims to persuade policymakers to prioritize zoning reform for climate resilience and equity.
